Lassi

Lassi



  • INGREDIENTS:

    • 2 cups Yoghurt
    • 2 tsps Sugar
    • 1 and 3/4 cups Chilled Water
    • 1 tsp Cardamom powder
    • pinch of salt
    • Ice Cubes



  • METHOD: 

    •           Add yoghurt, chilled water, cardamom powder,  sugar and salt in a blender.

          Blend it till it turns like a frothy texture.

          Pour in glasses in equal proportion.

          Add ice cubes in each glass and serve.
