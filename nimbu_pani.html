Nimbu Pani

Nimbu Pani



  • INGREDIENTS:

    • 1 Lemon
    • 400 ml of chilled water (2 glasses)
    • 1 tsp cumin powder (jeera powder)
    • 1 tsp chat masala
    • pinch of salt
    • 5 mint leaves
    • 2 tbsp sugar
    • Ice cubes
    • 2-4 Lemon Slices for decoration (optional)



  • METHOD: 

    •           In a bowl add the water.

          Squeeze the lemon and put the juice into the water.

          Dispose off the seeds.

          Add sugar, salt, cumin powder, chat masala & mint leaves.

          Mix until the sugar dissolves.

          Pour it into a tall glass and add the lemon slices in the water.

          Add the ice cubes.

          SERVE IT CHILLED
