Pav Bhaji

Pav Bhaji

  • INGREDIENTS 
      •                   
    • 3 boiled potatoes
      • 
                  
    • 4 finely chopped tomatoes
      • 
                  
    • 2 finely chopped onions
      • 
                  
    • 1 finely chopped carrot
      • 
                  
    • 2 finely chopped Capsicums
      • 
                  
    • 1 cup peas
      • 
                  
    • 200 gms finely chopped cauliflower
      • 
                  
    • 3 tbsp pav bhaji masala
      • 
                  
    • 1 tbsp chilli powder
      • 
                  
    • 1/2 tsp jeera powder
      • 
                  
    • 1 tsp turmeric powder
      • 
                  
    • 1 tsp jeera
      • 
                  
    • 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
      • 
                  
    • Salt
      • 
                  
    • 1 cup water
      • 
                  
    • 2 - 3 tbsp oil
      • 
                  
    • Lots of butter
      • 
                  
    • 1 onion for garnishing
      • 
                  
    • 1 tbsp corriander for garnishing
      • 
                  
    • 1 lemon
      •
  • METHOD 
    •             In a cooker boil the potatoes.....then remove them and add Capsicum, 
            peas, carrot, cauliflower and little bit of salt...
    
            Remove 6 whistles..
    
            Then mash the boiled potatoes with the boiled vegetables..
    
            In another container heat oil, little butter add the jeera, ginger garlic paste, onions and let the onions 
            cook..then add the tomatoes and let it get soft....then add chilli powder, turmeric powder, salt, butter, 1.5 tbsp 
            of pav bhaji masala and mix....then add the boiled vegetables and mix....then add the remaining 1.5 tbsp of pav bhaji 
            masala and mix....then add abt 3 tbsp of butter...add 1 cup of water and let it boil nicely....
    
            Serve hot with bread, onion, corriander and lemon..
    
            Fry the breads on a pan with butter.....
            

    
            SERVE HOT
