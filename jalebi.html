Jalebi



  • INGREDIENTS:

  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 4 tbsp curd
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Pinch of Salt
  • 2 tsp cardamom powder
  • Food colour (optional)
  • Water to make batter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1 tbsp jaggery powder
  • Oil for deep frying



  • METHOD:

    • Step 1: Sugar syrup 
              In a container heat 3/4 cup water then add the sugar and let the sugar dissolve.
          Then add the jaggery powder.
          Once it starts boiling switch off the flame.

    Step 2: Batter 
              In a bowl sieve maida, salt, food colour, cardamom powder & baking powder.
          Then add oil, curd and then add water and let it form a batter

    Step 3: Deep Frying 
              
          In a piping bag / zip-lock bag transfer the batter. Cut one of the edges.
          In a container heat oil nicely then slowly make shape of a jalebi and let it become golden brown.
          Then transfer it to the sugar syrup and let it be for 20-30 min in it.
