Medu Vada

Medu Vada

  • INGREDIENTS:

    • Soak 1 cup of washed urad dal in 3 cups of water overnight.
    • In a mixer add 3 tbsp of water and the soaked urad dal and grind till it forms a batter.
    • Grind in installments for 3 minutes.
      • BATTER SHOULD BE THICK
    • 1 tbsp chopped curry leaves
    • 2 chopped green chillies
    • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds (jeera)
    • pinch of asafoetida (hing)
    • 1/2 tsp pepper powder
    • salt as per taste
    • 3 tbsp chopped corriander leaves.
    • 1/4 cup grated coconut


  • METHOD: 

    •           Mix all the ingredients mentioned in the ingredients section in the thick batter.
          
          Apply oil on the palm.
          
          Take a portion of dough on your palm and roll it into a ball.
          
          Flatten it a bit and make a hole in the center with the help of your finger.
          
          Deep fry it in hot oil.
          
          Repeat this process till the batter is over.
