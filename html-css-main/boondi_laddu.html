Boondi Laddu

Boondi Laddu



  • INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 cup besan
  • Water for batter
  • 3/4 sugar
  • 1 & 1/4 cup water
  • 1 tsp jaggery powder
  • 1 tsp cardamom powder(elaichi)
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon powder
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla essence (optional)
  • Cashew Nuts 4-5 tbsp



  • METHOD:

    • For Sugar Syrup: 
              In a container heat cup water then add the sugar and let the sugar dissolve.
          
          Then add the jaggery powder.
          
          Once it starts boiling switch off the flame.
    Making: 
              In a bowl mix besan and water and make a thick batter.
          
          With the help of a spoon put small portions of the batter in the oil and let it fry.

          Once fried remove in a plate and let it cool.

          Transfer the fried besan in a mixer and grind to coarse.

          Once done add the powder in the sugar syrup and mix them together on low flame.

          Add the cardamom powder, cinnamon powder, vanilla essence & cashew seeds and mix thoroughly on low flame.

          Turn off the flame and let it cool.

          Once cooled make round balls and enjoy !!!
