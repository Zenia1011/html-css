Idli




  • INGREDIENTS:
    • 1 cup parboiled rice
    • 1 cup regular rice
    • Rinse both the rice
    • 1/4 cup thick poha
    • Rinse the poha
    • 1/2 cups urad dal (husked black gram)
    • 1/4 tsp Fenugreek seeds(optional)
    • 1 tsp salt
    • Oil to Greece the idli mould


  • METHOD: 
    •           Add the poha to the rice.
          Add 2 cups water
          Mix and keep aside for 4-5 hours

          In a bowl add the urad dal and Fenugreek seeds
          Rinse them a couple of times
          Add 1 cup water & soak for 4-5 hours

          Drain the water from the urad dal but don't throw the water

          Blend the rice and lentils with the water kept aside 

          Grind the urad dal for some seconds by adding the soaked water

          Pour the urad dal batter in a pan/bowl

          Drain the water from rice and poha
          Grind it to paste
          Add the poha and rice batter into the urad dal bowl

          Add salt and mix well

          Keep the batter overnight for fermentation.

          Greece the idli mould with oil.
          Gently add and swirl the batter.
          With a round spoon pour portions of the batter in the greased idli moulds.

          Steam them for 12-15 mins.
          After that insert a knife and check if it's cooked.
          Keep it for some more time if the knife does not come out clean
          Serve them with sambhar and coconut chutney.
