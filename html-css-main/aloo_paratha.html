Aloo Paratha

Aloo Paratha

  • INGREDIENTS 
      •               
    • 3 boiled potatoes
      • 
              
    • Water as required to Knead the dough
      • 
              
    • 3 cups maida
      • 
              
    • 1 finely chopped onion
      • 
              
    • 4 cloves of chopped garlic
      • 
              
    • 1 tsp Finely Chopped corriander
      • 
              
    • 1 tsp garam masala powder
      • 
              
    • 1 & 1/2 tsp Chilli powder
      • 
              
    • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
      • 
              
    • 1/2 tsp jeera powder
      • 
              
    • 1/4 tsp chat masala
      • 
              
    • Oil for frying the parathas
      •
  • METHOD 
    •           In a bowl add the maida, boiled potato, onion, corriander, garlic, garam masala powder, chilli powder, turmeric 
          powder, jeera powder, chat masala. Add water and make dough.

          Then make small portions. 

          Roll them and fry like chapatis.


          

    
          
          Note: U can even use butter or ghee for frying
