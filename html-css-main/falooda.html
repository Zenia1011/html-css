Falooda

Falooda



  • INGREDIENTS:

    • 2 cups milk
    • 4 tbsp jelly
    • 1 tbsp falooda seeds
    • 7 sliced pista & almonds (badam)
    • 4 cherries(optional but recommended)
    • 1/4 cup sugar
    • 4 tbsp rose syrup
    • falooda sev



  • METHOD: 

    •           In a container boil the milk.

          Add the falooda sev and bring to a boil.

          Reduce the flame.

          Add the sugar and the rose syrup and mix.

          Once it starts thickening, turn off the flame.

          Soak falooda seeds in water in a bowl.

          Let it get soaked well.

          Place the serving glass in the freezer for 1hr.

          Pour 1/4 tbsp of rose syrup in the chilled glass.

          Add 2 tbsp of jelly.

          Add 2 tbsp of soaked falooda seeds in the glass.

          Pour 1 tbsp of rose syrup.

          Add 1/4 cup of falooda sev.

          Pour the milk.

          Garnish with almonds, pista and cherries and serve chilled.
