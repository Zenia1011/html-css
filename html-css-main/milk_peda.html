Jalebi

Jalebi



  • INGREDIENTS:

    • 1.5 litre milk
    • 1/4cup + 3tbsp powdered sugar
    • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
    • 1/4 tsp cinnamon powder
    • 1/2 tsp vanilla essence
    • Pista nuts / Almonds (Badam)



  • METHOD: 

    •         Heat a wok.
        
        Add the milk 
        
        Bring the milk to a boil and keep stiring
        
        Keep stiring till it gets a thick consistency.
        
        Keep stiring the sides so that it dosent stick.
        
        This process takes about 40-45 mins.
        
        When it changes coloye to pale yellow reduce the flame.
        
        Keep stiring.
        
        Add the powdered sugar and mix thouroughly.
        
        Mix for some 2 mins till sugar gets dissolved.
        
        Add cardamom powder, sugar powder & vanilla essence.
        
        Transfer it in a plate and let it cool.
        
        Once cooled make small balls and flatten them.
        
        Add a pista chopped into half in the centre of the peda for a design.
