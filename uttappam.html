Uttappam

Uttappam




  • INGREDIENTS:

      • For Dosa Batter 
                  
    • 1/2 cup urad dal
      • 
            
    • 3 tbsp chana dal
      • 
            
    • 1/2 tsp methi seeds
      • 
            
    • 3 tbsps poha in an other container.
      • 
            
    • 1 & 1/2 cup washed raw rice and soaked in water for 5-6hrs
      •
      For Toppings 
                  
    • 1 finely chopped onion
      • 
            
    • 1 finely chopped corriander
      • 
            
    • 2 tbsp corriander
      • 
            
    • 3-4 tbsp red chilli powder in a bowl
      • 
            
    • ghee or oil
      •



  • METHOD: 

    •           Wash and soak the urad dal & chana dal 30mins before grinding.
          
          In a separate conainer wash & soak the poha 30mins before grinding.
          
          In a mixer, add 1 & 1/4 cup water and 1/2 tsp salt.
          
          Add the dal & poha.
          
          Blend them to a frothy and bubbly batter.
          
          Transfer it to a bowl and set aside.
          
          Slightly coarse grind the rice with 1/2 cup of water (semolina texture).
          
          If needed pour 1/4 cup water and blend.
          
          Transfer it into the dal & poha batter bowl.
          
          Mix everything well and allow it to ferment overnight.
          
          In a pan heat abt a tsp of oil.
          
          When the oil is hot enough, pour a big spoon of batter on the center of the pan and quickly spread it into a round shape.
          
          Add oil/butter on the edges.
          
          Flip the Dosa
          
          In a bowl mix all the ingredients mentioned in the Toppings section. 
          
          Now spread some ghee on the flipped Dosa.
          
          Sprinkle some chilli powder.  
          
          Now add about 1 tsp of the Toppings on the batter on the dosa and let it cook for 2 mins on low flame.
          
          Repeat till the the dosa batter and toppings get over.
