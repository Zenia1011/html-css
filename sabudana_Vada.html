Sabudana Vada

Sabudana Vada




  • INGREDIENTS:

    • 1 cup Soaked sabudana (overnight)
    • 1 tsp mustard seeds (rai)
    • 1 tsp cumin seeds (jeera)
    • 3 curry leaves
    • 2 finely chopped green chillies
    • 1 tsp finely chopped/grated ginger
    • 1 finely chopped onion
    • 1 finely chopped tomato
    • 1 boiled potato
    • 1 tsp chilli powder
    • 1/4 cup roasted peanuts
    • 1 tbsp chopped corriander leaves
    • 1 tsp lemon juice
    • 6 tbsp rice flour
    • 6 tbsp semolina (rawa)
    • salt as per taste



  • METHOD: 

    •           In a pan heat abt 2 tbsp of oil.
          
          Add mustard seeds and let them crackle.
          
          Add cumin seeds, curry leaves, green chillies.
          
          Add the ginger.
          
          Add the peanuts and saute.
          
          Add the chopped onion.
          
          Let the onions get transparent.
          
          Add the tomato and saute.
          
          Add the chilli powder and salt.
          
          Add the boiled & mashed potato and saute.
          
          Add the soaked sabudana & mix thouroughly.
          
          In the end add the lemon juice and corriander.
          
          Switch off the flame and let it cool down.
          

          
          Make small portions of the cooked sambudana.
          
          Make round balls of each portion and flatten them slightly.
          
          In a plate mix the rice flour and semolina.
          
          Heat oil.
          
          Coat the flattened sabudana balls in rice flour and semolina mixture.
          
          You can either deep fry or shallow fry them as per your wish..
