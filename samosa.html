Samosa

Samosa



  • INGRDIENTS:

      • FOR FILLING
    • 4 potatoes boiled & mashed
    • 2-3 tbsp oil
    • 1 tsp mustard seeds(rai)
    • 1 tsp cumin seeds (jeera)
    • 1 & 1/2 tsp urad dal (optional)
    • 1 finely chopped onion
    • 3/4 cup green peas
    • 2 chopped green chillies
    • 1 tsp grated ginger
    • 12 chopped curry leaves
    • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
    • 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
    • pinch of asafoetida (hing)
    • 1/4 cup chopped corriander
    • 1/3 cup water
    • salt as per taste

      • FOR DOUGH
    • 2 cups maida
    • 1 tsp ajwain
    • 1/2 tsp salt
    • 1/4 cup ghee/oil
    • Water to knead the dough (if required)


  • Method: 

    •           FOR FILLING
          In a container heat oil.
          Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds and let them sizzle.
          Add the curry leaves, urad dal, green chillies & ginger.
          Add the onion and cook till the onions turn transparent.
          Add the peas.
          Add the turmeric powder, chilli powder and mix.
          Add the boiled & mashed potatoes and mix.
          Cover and cook for 2 mins on low flame.
          Add the water and salt and saute.
          Cover and cook for 5 minutes.
        
     
         FOR DOUGH
          In a bowl mix all the ingredients mentioned in the Dough section.
          Knead till it forms a dough.
          Let it rest for 30mins.
          Divide in 5-6 equal portions.
          Roll into an oval shape.
          Cut from center to make 2 equal parts.
          Apply water over the straight edge with your finger.
          Join the edges and make a cone shape.
          Add the potato filling.
          Apply water on the edges and stick to seal them.

          Deep fry them in hot oil till they turn slight brown.
